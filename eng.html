“Balakhani Operating Company LTD” 
History

1594

According to an inscription on a stone found in one of the oil wells in Balakhani (35 m deep), it was dug and put into use in 1594 by the sculptor Allahyar Muhammadnur oglu.

1859

The first oil refinery was built in Baku.

1871

Agarza Mirzayev laid the foundation for industrial drilling of wells in Balakhani.

1872

In the first auction of oil fields, 15 fields from Balakhani and 2 fields from Bibiheybet were put up for sale.

1878

The world's first operational oil pipeline connected Balakhani Mines with oil refining facilities in Kara Shahar. The belt, equipped with steam-powered pumps, was 9 km long. The arch was built by engineer-architect Vladimir Shukhov, who was invited to Baku by Nobels.

1898

A silent film called "Oil fountain in the Balakhani mine" was shot by the photographer Alexander Mishon. This film laid the groundwork for the creation of Azerbaijani cinematography and remained the first short documentary film about oil in the world cinematography.
